Mission

Our mission is to breed and raise exceptional dogs that will bring joy, love, and companionship to their new families. We strive to provide the best possible service to our clients, including educating them about responsible dog ownership and helping them find the perfect match for their lifestyle.

Vission

Our vision is to be recognized as a leading kennel, known for our commitment to breeding and raising exceptional dogs and providing outstanding customer service. We aim to continue to improve the quality of our dogs through careful breeding, while always prioritizing their health, temperament, and well-being. We hope to inspire responsible dog ownership and make a positive impact on the lives of both our clients and our furry friends.

How we do it

  • We carefully select parent dogs based on their health, temperament, and conformation to breed standards.
  • We prioritize dogs with good temperaments and avoid any breeding practices that are unethical or harmful to dogs.
  • Our breeding program is designed to produce healthy and happy puppies with excellent temperaments.
  • We work closely with our veterinarian to ensure that our dogs receive the best possible care throughout the breeding process and beyond.

Care and Socialization

Comfortable Living Conditions

Our dogs live in clean, comfortable, and spacious kennels that are designed to meet their physical and emotional needs. We provide our dogs with plenty of opportunities for exercise and play, including outdoor play areas and regular walks.

Socialization

Socialization is an essential part of a dog's development, and we make sure that our dogs receive plenty of socialization from an early age. We expose our puppies to different people, environments, and experiences to help them develop into well-adjusted and confident dogs. We also encourage our clients to continue socializing their puppies after they go home to help them adjust to new situations and people

Veterinary Care

Our company prioritizes the health of our dogs by providing regular veterinary care, including check-ups, vaccinations, parasite prevention, and emergency care. We also offer health guarantees for our dogs. We are commitment to high-quality veterinary care.
