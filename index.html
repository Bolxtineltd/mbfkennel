Our Services

We offer a wide range of services to meet your dog-related needs, including

Dog Sales

Our kennel is home to a variety of breeds, including but not limited to Labrador Retrievers, German Shepherds, Rottweilers, Doberman Pinschers, and Belgian Malinois. We only offer dogs that meet our strict standards for health, temperament, and behavior, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible pet or security dog for their specific needs.

Dog Mating

Our kennel is equipped to provide dog mating services to breed healthy and well-tempered puppies. Our experienced staff oversees the entire process, from initial breeding to the birth of puppies, ensuring that both the mother and puppies receive the best possible care.

Dog Training

Our team consists of experienced and professional dog trainers who can provide basic obedience training, advanced training for protection or hunting, and everything in between. We take a personalized approach to dog training, tailoring our services to meet the unique needs of each individual dog and owner.
